# Consolidate the dashboard top region into one focused card below the "On track" status banner.

## Why
The current top region has three surfaces all saying "you're fine" in different visual languages: the standalone priority pills row, the "On track" banner, and the row of four stat cards. We're collapsing the redundancy. The "On track" banner keeps doing reassurance; one new card below it does celebration + triage.

## What to remove
- The standalone priority pills row at the top of the page (3 Urgent / 9 Medium / 47 Low). It moves into the new card.
- The four-stat-card row (Emails safely deferred / Tasks scheduled / Unsafe deferrals / Missed deadlines). The numbers don't disappear — see Data contract.

## What to keep, unchanged
- The greeting header ("Good morning, Dr. Morgan…")
- The "On track" + "AI Recommendation" two-column status banner
- Today's plan and My tasks sections

## What to add
One new card directly below the "On track" banner. White background, 0.5px tertiary border, 12px radius, ~24px 28px padding. Two stacked sections separated by a 0.5px top divider.

### Section 1 — hero
- 72px number, weight 500, tight line-height, slight negative letter-spacing — bound to `weeklyDeferredCount`
- Inline on the same baseline: "emails handled for you this week" at 22px / weight 500
- Below, in secondary text colour at 14px: Tabler `clock` icon + "≈ {hoursSaved} hours of inbox time saved"
- `hoursSaved = Math.round(weeklyDeferredCount * 5 / 60)` — placeholder formula (~5 min per email); confirm the constant with me before ship

### Section 2 — priority triage
- 14px secondary-text label: "Pending, by priority"
- Three rounded-full pills in a row, 8px gap, ~5px 14px padding, 13px text:
  - `{urgent}` "Urgent" — danger background token, danger text token
  - `{medium}` "Medium" — warning background token, warning text token
  - `{low}` "Low" — secondary background token; primary text on the number, secondary text on the label
- Number is weight 500, label is weight 400. No icons inside the pills.

## Copy is locked
Use these strings exactly. Don't paraphrase, don't title-case, don't reorder:
- "emails handled for you this week"
- "≈ {n} hours of inbox time saved"
- "Pending, by priority"
- "Urgent" / "Medium" / "Low"

## Data contract
- `weeklyDeferredCount: number` → hero number, also drives hoursSaved
- `pending.urgent`, `pending.medium`, `pending.low: number` → priority pills
- `tasksScheduled`, `unsafeDeferrals`, `missedDeadlines` — these don't get a home in the new card. They belong in the "On track" banner above. If that banner doesn't already surface them, flag back to me before deleting them from the page.

## States to handle
- **Start of week** (`weeklyDeferredCount === 0`): "0 / ≈ 0 hours saved" falls flat. Flag back to me which you'd prefer — (a) show honest zeros as-is, or (b) swap to a forward-looking variant on Mondays.
- **Empty priority bucket** (any of urgent/medium/low === 0): still show the pill at 0. "0 Urgent" is reassuring — the whole point of the row is triage.
- **Issues present** (`unsafeDeferrals > 0` or `missedDeadlines > 0`): this card stays neutral. The "On track" banner above is responsible for changing tone. Don't dual-purpose this card.

## Don'ts
- No gradients, shadows, or extra colour accents
- No title case, no mid-sentence bolding
- Don't reintroduce the four stat cards anywhere
- Don't keep the top priority pills row — delete the container, don't just hide it
